# Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** August 1, 2025 Welcome to SpokeCircle. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the SpokeCircle mobile app, you agree to this policy. ## 1. What We Collect We only collect what's needed to help you join and host group rides: ### Anonymous Usage (default) - Location (used once for finding nearby rides, never stored) - Ride participation data (joined, created) - Device information (type, OS, app version) ### If You Create an Optional Profile - Display name / username - Ride history (hosted or joined) - Optional avatar or profile photo ### If You Contact Us - Email address - Any information you provide in your message ## 2. How We Use Your Data We use your data to: - Help users find and join local rides - Show relevant events based on location - Improve app functionality and user experience - Prevent abuse or spam **We do NOT sell your data. Ever.** ## 3. Third-Party Services We may use: - **Google Maps API** – to show ride locations - **Crashlytics / Firebase** – for bug reporting and performance - **Email tools** (e.g., SendGrid or Mailgun) – only if you email us All third-party services comply with modern data protection standards. ## 4. Data Retention - Anonymous ride data is kept for analytics - Profile data is retained until you delete your account - You can delete all your data at any time by emailing us at: **privacy@spokecircle.com** ## 5. International Users (GDPR) If you're in the EU, you have rights: - Access to your data - Request deletion - Limit processing Email privacy@spokecircle.com with your request. We'll respond within 30 days. ## 6. CCPA (California Residents) You have the right to: - Know what we collect - Request deletion - Opt-out of sale (we don't sell anything) Contact us at privacy@spokecircle.com for CCPA-specific requests. ## 7.
