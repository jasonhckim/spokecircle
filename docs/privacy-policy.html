Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

Welcome to SpokeCircle. Your privacy is important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the SpokeCircle mobile app, you agree to this policy.

1. What We Collect

We only collect what's needed to help you join and host group rides:

Anonymous Usage (default)

  • Location (used once for finding nearby rides, never stored)
  • Ride participation data (joined, created)
  • Device information (type, OS, app version)

If You Create an Optional Profile

  • Display name / username
  • Ride history (hosted or joined)
  • Optional avatar or profile photo

If You Contact Us

  • Email address
  • Any information you provide in your message

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Help users find and join local rides
  • Show relevant events based on location
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Prevent abuse or spam
We do NOT sell your data. Ever.
